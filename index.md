## Islamic Values & Daily Life

Faith is the foundation of everything at Hidayah. The school's five Islamic pillars are:

1. **Iman (Faith)** — Quran & Tajweed from Pre-KG, building a lifelong bond with the word of Allah
2. **Akhlaq (Character)** — Dedicated Tarbiyat sessions on honesty, responsibility, compassion, and leadership
3. **Ibadah (Worship)** — Salah training, wudu practice, Islamic manners (Adab) built into school life
4. **Ilm (Knowledge)** — A structured Islamic Studies syllabus covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Hadith, Seerah
5. **Khidmah (Service)** — Community service and peer accountability programmes

### A Typical School Day
- **Morning** — Assembly with Duas
- **Early Session** — Quran / Arabic class
- **Main Session** — CBSE core subjects
- **Break** — Lunch + Dhuhr Salah
- **Post-Lunch** — Islamic Studies / Tarbiyat
- **Activity Period** — Sports, Arts, or Clubs
- **Dismissal** — Closing Duas & Dhikr

## Admissions

Admissions are open for the academic year 2026–27. The school accepts applications for:

### Admission Process
1. **Collect Form** — Download or pick up from school office
2. **Submit Form** — Return with required documents
3. **Assessment** — Scholarship Entrance Assessment & student interaction
4. **Confirmation** — Receive admission status from the school
5. **Fee & Enrolment** — Complete payment and finalise admission

### Required Documents
- Birth Certificate (original for verification)
- Passport-size photos (child + both parents, 4 each)
