About Hidayah International School

A school founded on faith, driven by excellence, and committed to raising the next generation of Muslim leaders.

Our Story

Who We Are

Hidayah International School is a CBSE-affiliated institution nestled in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Founded on the conviction that education must nourish both the intellect and the soul, HIS provides a unique learning environment where academic excellence and Islamic values are inseparable.

From Pre-KG through Class VII, our students are guided by dedicated educators trained in both CBSE pedagogy and Islamic education — ensuring that every lesson, every interaction, and every experience shapes them into well-rounded, purpose-driven individuals.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Success for Both Worlds

To be a leading institution that nurtures academically excellent, Islamically grounded, and socially responsible individuals — equipped to lead with integrity in every sphere of life, in this world and the next.

Our Mission

Educate. Inspire. Transform.

To provide a holistic education that integrates CBSE academic rigour with authentic Islamic values — through qualified teachers, a nurturing environment, and programmes that develop the mind, heart, and character of every child.
