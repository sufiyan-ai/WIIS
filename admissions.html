Admissions Overview

Why Apply to Hidayah?

Hidayah International School welcomes families who seek quality CBSE education grounded in authentic Islamic values. We believe every child deserves an environment where their faith and intellect are equally nurtured.

Admissions are based on an interaction and assessment process to ensure the best fit for your child. Seats are limited — we encourage early applications.

✓Admissions open for Academic Year 2026–27
✓Limited seats available across all grades
✓Admissions based on interaction / assessment
✓Scholarship Entrance Assessment available

Classes Available

🌱 Beginners (Pre-KG)
📚 Level 1 (KG)
🎓 Level 2 (UKG)
📖 Grade 1 to Grade 6B

⚠️ Limited seats — early applications are strongly recommended.

Required Documents

What to Bring

Please ensure you have the following documents ready when submitting the admission form.

Birth Certificate copy (original for verification)
Passport size photos — child + both parents (4 each)
Aadhar card copy — child and parent
Transfer Certificate (required for Grade 2 and above)
Previous year's report card / progress report
Community certificate (if applicable)
