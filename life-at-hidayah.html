Beyond the Bell

Life at Hidayah

Where students learn, play, create — and grow. School life at Hidayah is rich, purposeful, and unforgettable.

Student Activities

Thriving Beyond the Classroom

A rich calendar of sports, arts, clubs, and trips that shape confident, well-rounded students.

Sports & Athletics

Cricket, football, kabaddi, and more — our sports programme teaches teamwork, discipline, and resilience on and off the field.

Arts & Crafts

Creative expression rooted in Islamic aesthetics. Students explore art, calligraphy, and crafts that connect creativity to culture.

Islamic Clubs & Nasheeds

Nasheed clubs, Quran circles, and Islamic knowledge groups that nurture identity and love of deen through shared expression.

Academic Clubs & Olympiads

Science, Math, and Quran Olympiads — intellectual competitions that build confidence and academic excellence beyond the classroom.

School Trips

Annual educational and nature trips that connect classroom learning to real-world experiences and broaden students' horizons.
