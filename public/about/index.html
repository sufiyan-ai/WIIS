About Hidayah

A school where CBSE excellence meets Islamic values — raising confident, compassionate, and capable students.

Welcome to HIS

A School with Purpose, Values & Vision

"Educating for success in both worlds — through knowledge, character, and faith."

Hidayah International School is a CBSE (Under Registration) institution in Bengaluru that integrates modern education with authentic Islamic values. We believe that true education encompasses the mind, heart, and soul.

Our students grow into confident, compassionate, and purpose-driven individuals — ready to lead with integrity in every sphere of life.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

🎯

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures students to become principled, knowledgeable, and compassionate leaders who contribute positively to society while staying rooted in Islamic values.

📜

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that combines rigorous CBSE academics with authentic Islamic teachings, fostering intellectual, moral, and spiritual growth in every student.

💡

Our Philosophy

We believe that education is not just about grades — it's about character. Every child deserves an environment where academic excellence and moral development go hand in hand.

What We Stand For

Core Values

🕌

Islamic Identity
