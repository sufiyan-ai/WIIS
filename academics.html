Curriculum

CBSE — A National Standard of Excellence

Hidayah International School follows the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum — India's most trusted and widely recognised educational framework. Our academic programme runs from Pre-KG through Class VII, giving your child a strong foundation across all core subjects.

What makes Hidayah unique is how Islamic values and Quranic studies are woven alongside this national curriculum — not as an add-on, but as an integral part of daily school life.

Teaching Approach

How We Teach

Our educators use evidence-based CBSE methodology enriched with Islamic wisdom and modern tools.

1

Activity-Based Learning

Hands-on projects, experiments, and collaborative work that make learning tangible and memorable.

2

Smart Classroom Technology

Smart TVs and interactive tools in every classroom to bring lessons to life through visual and interactive content.

3

Islamic Values Integration

Quran, Hadith, and Islamic ethics are naturally woven into lessons — not treated as separate subjects.

4

Individualised Attention

Small class sizes allow teachers to identify each student's strengths and give targeted support.

5

Regular Parent Communication

Frequent updates, parent meetings, and a dedicated family program keep parents engaged and informed.

6

Bilingual Instruction

Instruction in English with Arabic and Urdu/Kannada support to ensure every child can follow along.
